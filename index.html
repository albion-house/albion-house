ABOUT ALBION HOUSE
ALBION HOUSE IS AN INDEPENDENT GAME STUDIO
FOUNDED BY TWO COUSINS.
GROWING UP, WE LIVED IN DIFFERENT CITIES
BUT SPENT OUR SUMMERS TOGETHER AT OUR
GRANDPARENTS' HOUSE IN ALBION, NEBRASKA.
IT WAS A HUGE OLD HOUSE, BEAUTIFUL IN
PLACES AND FORGOTTEN IN OTHERS, FILLED
WITH WINDING HALLWAYS, HIDDEN ROOMS, AND
ENOUGH MYSTERIES TO KEEP TWO KIDS
EXPLORING ALL SUMMER LONG.
WE PLAYED VIDEO GAMES TOGETHER IN THE
BASEMENT, WHERE WE DISCOVERED AN OLD
APPLE IIE AND LEARNED TO MAKE OUR OWN.
THOSE LONG SUMMER AFTERNOONS DISAPPEARED
INTO IMAGINING IMPOSSIBLE WONDERS,
INVENTIONS, SOLUTIONS, AND
ASKING EACH OTHER, "WHAT IF?"
ONE COUSIN PURSUED ART. THE OTHER
PURSUED COMPUTER SCIENCE.
YEARS LATER, WE TEAMED UP AGAIN TO MAKE
GAMES TOGETHER.
WE'RE STILL ASKING, "WHAT IF?"

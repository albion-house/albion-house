ABOUT ALBION HOUSE
ALBION HOUSE IS AN INDEPENDENT GAME STUDIO
FOUNDED BY TWO COUSINS.
GROWING UP, WE LIVED IN DIFFERENT CITIES
BUT SPENT OUR SUMMERS TOGETHER AT OUR
GRANDPARENTS' HOUSE IN ALBION, NEBRASKA.
IT WAS A BEAUTIFUL OLD PLACE FILLED WITH
WINDING HALLWAYS, HIDDEN ROOMS, AND
ENOUGH MYSTERIES FOR TWO KIDS TO SPEND
AN ENTIRE SUMMER EXPLORING.
WE PLAYED VIDEO GAMES TOGETHER IN THE
BASEMENT, LEARNED TO MAKE OUR OWN ON AN
APPLE IIE, AND SPENT COUNTLESS HOURS
EXPLORING, IMAGINING, AND ASKING,
"WHAT IF?"
EVENTUALLY, ONE COUSIN WENT TO ART SCHOOL.
THE OTHER STUDIED COMPUTER SCIENCE.
YEARS LATER, THOSE PATHS CAME BACK TOGETHER.
ALBION HOUSE IS NAMED AFTER THE PLACE
WHERE IT ALL STARTED.

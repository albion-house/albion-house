ABOUT ALBION HOUSE
ALBION HOUSE IS AN INDEPENDENT GAME STUDIO FOUNDED BY TWO COUSINS.
GROWING UP, WE LIVED IN DIFFERENT CITIES BUT SPENT OUR SUMMERS TOGETHER AT OUR GRANDPARENTS' HOUSE IN ALBION, NEBRASKA. IT WAS A BEAUTIFUL OLD PLACE THAT SEEMED FULL OF MYSTERIES—ORNATE ROOMS, FORGOTTEN CORNERS, AND ENOUGH SPACE FOR TWO KIDS TO BELIEVE THERE WAS ALWAYS SOMETHING NEW LEFT TO DISCOVER.
WE PLAYED GAMES IN THE BASEMENT, LEARNED TO MAKE OUR OWN ON AN APPLE IIE, AND SPENT COUNTLESS HOURS EXPLORING, IMAGINING, AND ASKING, "WHAT IF?"
EVENTUALLY, ONE COUSIN WENT TO ART SCHOOL. THE OTHER STUDIED COMPUTER SCIENCE.
YEARS LATER, THOSE PATHS CAME BACK TOGETHER.
ALBION HOUSE IS NAMED AFTER THE PLACE WHERE IT ALL STARTED. THE QUESTIONS THAT STARTED THERE ARE STILL GUIDING US TODAY.
